Governance Charter
Investor Conduct and Communication
SD-INV-GOV-2026
Version: 1.0
Effective: April 2026
This charter sets baseline expectations for investor communication, confidentiality, and governance etiquette within Swift Designz investor operations.
Communication Protocol
  • Operational queries should be directed through approved investor communication channels.
  • Response windows may vary based on urgency and reporting cycle timing.
  • Material concerns should be escalated in writing with clear context.
Confidentiality Discipline
All portal content remains confidential under the Investor NDA. Sharing internal metrics or sensitive records without authorization is prohibited.
Decision Boundaries
Unless contractually specified, portal access does not grant direct operational control. Strategic input is considered through agreed governance channels.
Review Cadence
This charter may be updated as governance maturity evolves. Updated versions are published in the Documents module.
