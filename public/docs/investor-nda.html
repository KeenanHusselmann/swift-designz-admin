This Investor Non-Disclosure Agreement applies to all information available within the Swift Designz Investor Portal and any related investor communications.
Confidential Information
Confidential Information includes business strategy, financial performance, forecasts, pricing models, client references, project details, internal systems, and any non-public operational content.
Investor Obligations
The investor agrees to:
  • Keep all confidential information private and secure.
  • Use confidential information only for legitimate investor oversight and communication.
  • Not distribute portal exports, screenshots, or reports to unauthorized third parties.
  • Promptly notify Swift Designz of any suspected data leak or unauthorized access.
Exclusions
Confidentiality obligations do not apply to information that is already public through no breach by the investor, or information required by law to be disclosed where legal notice can be provided.
Term
Confidentiality obligations remain active for the duration of investor access and for 24 months after access ends.
Remedies
Breach of this agreement may result in immediate access revocation and may require legal or equitable remedies to protect confidential information.
