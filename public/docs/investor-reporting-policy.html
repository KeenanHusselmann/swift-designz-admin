Version: 1.0
Effective: April 2026
This policy defines how Swift Designz provides operational and financial visibility to investors through the Investor Portal.
Reporting Frequency
  • Dashboard metrics are updated as new records are captured.
  • Formal investor statements are generated monthly unless otherwise agreed.
  • Material operational changes are communicated through official channels.
Included Data
Portfolio updates may include project progress, invoice and payment snapshots, investor contributions, and approved equipment visibility.
Limitations
Reporting reflects available internal records and management estimates where applicable. It is informational and does not replace regulated legal, audit, tax, or investment advice.
Corrections
If an investor identifies a discrepancy, Swift Designz will review and issue corrections in the next reporting cycle or sooner when material.
